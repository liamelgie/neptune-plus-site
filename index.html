How It Works

What is Plex?

Plex is an app that allows users to stream their films and tv shows to their various devices, including TVs, phones/tablets and games consoles.

By joining Neptune, you can access our massive library and stream it to your heart's content without having to do any of the hard work yourself. Phew.

Request an Invite

Create an account on Plex.tv and log in. Go to this page and click 'Invite Friend'.

Type in 'Neptune Plus' and click continue. You'll know you got the right account if you see our logo and a green check mark. Click continue a few more times and then click send.

Stream your heart out

We'll recieve your request and once it's accepted, you'll get an email letting you know that you're ready to go!

If you don't hear anything after 24 hours, send us a message on Discord and we'll get right to it.

Remux

A remux is a perfect copy of the blu-ray release you'd buy at shop or online, converted to a friendly format that you can stream to your TV or web browser. No compression and no compromises. You won't find a higher quality HD stream, anywhere.

These high quality rips require a faster connection than normal (around 30mbp/s) to stream but are definitely worth it.

4K

4K/HDR content lets your fancy new TV shine with 4x the resolution of HD and truer to life colours. Not all content is available in 4K/HDR but we're sure to grab every last one, the moment it's released.

4K requires A LOT of bandwidth so make sure your connection is up to scratch. 70mbp/s or more is an absolute must. Your TV must support HDR or else it will not display correctly.
